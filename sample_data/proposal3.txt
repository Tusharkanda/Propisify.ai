Executive Summary
FinServe is expanding its digital banking platform to better serve its growing customer base. Our software development team will deliver a secure, user-friendly mobile banking solution, leveraging industry best practices and robust security protocols. This proposal follows a standardized template for clarity and consistency.

Scope of Work
Phase 1: Requirements & Planning (Weeks 1-2)
- Stakeholder interviews and requirements gathering
- Technical assessment and architecture planning
- Deliverable: Requirements Specification Document

Phase 2: Mobile App Development (Weeks 3-10)
- UI/UX design and prototyping
- Core banking feature development (account management, transfers, bill pay)
- Integration with existing backend systems
- Deliverable: Beta version of mobile app

Phase 3: Security Testing & Compliance (Weeks 11-12)
- Penetration testing and vulnerability assessment
- Compliance checks (PCI DSS, GDPR)
- Deliverable: Security & Compliance Report

Phase 4: User Acceptance & Launch (Weeks 13-14)
- User acceptance testing (UAT)
- Bug fixes and final optimizations
- App store deployment and go-live support
- Deliverable: Production-ready mobile app
