Parties
FinServe Bank ("FinServe") and InsightData Analytics LLC ("InsightData") (collectively, the "Parties") enter into this Agreement as of September 1, 2025.

1. Term
This Agreement commences on September 1, 2025 and continues until August 31, 2026 unless terminated earlier as provided herein.

2. Scope of Work / Services
InsightData will provide advanced data analytics consulting and dashboard development for FinServe's banking operations.

3. Payment Terms
FinServe will pay InsightData $80,000 in two equal installments of $40,000, due at project start and upon delivery of final dashboards.

4. Confidentiality
Both Parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all financial and customer data disclosed during the term of this Agreement and for five years thereafter.

5. Intellectual Property
All analytics models and dashboards developed under this Agreement shall be owned by FinServe.

6. Warranties and Representations
Each Party represents and warrants that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement and will comply with all applicable financial regulations.

7. Limitation of Liability
Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. InsightData's total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid.

8. Termination
This Agreement may be terminated by either Party upon 45 days' written notice or for material breach not cured within 15 days.

9. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes shall be resolved by arbitration in Boston, MA.

10. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

11. Miscellaneous
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
