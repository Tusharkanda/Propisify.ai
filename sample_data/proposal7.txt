Executive Summary
EduSmart is launching a new online learning platform to expand its digital education offerings. Our development team will deliver a scalable, interactive platform with advanced analytics and personalized learning paths. This proposal follows a standardized template for clarity and consistency.

Scope of Work
Phase 1: Discovery & Design (Weeks 1-2)
- Requirements gathering and stakeholder interviews
- Platform architecture and UI/UX design
- Deliverable: Design Specification Document

Phase 2: Platform Development (Weeks 3-8)
- Core platform and content management system development
- Integration of video streaming and interactive modules
- Deliverable: Beta version of learning platform

Phase 3: Analytics & Personalization (Weeks 9-10)
- Implementation of analytics dashboard
- Development of personalized learning path algorithms
- Deliverable: Analytics dashboard and personalization features

Phase 4: Testing & Launch (Weeks 11-12)
- User acceptance testing (UAT)
- Bug fixes and deployment
- Deliverable: Production-ready platform
