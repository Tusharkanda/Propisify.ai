Parties
MediHealth, Inc. ("MediHealth") and DataCare Solutions LLC ("DataCare") (collectively, the "Parties") enter into this Agreement as of July 1, 2025.

1. Term
This Agreement commences on July 1, 2025 and continues until June 30, 2026 unless terminated earlier as provided herein.

2. Scope of Work / Services
DataCare will provide a cloud-based analytics SaaS platform and related support services to MediHealth, as described in Exhibit A.

3. Payment Terms
MediHealth will pay DataCare $5,000 per month, invoiced at the start of each month. Additional services will be billed at $150/hour.

4. Confidentiality
Both Parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all patient and business data disclosed during the term of this Agreement and for three years thereafter.

5. Intellectual Property
All intellectual property developed under this Agreement shall be owned by DataCare, except for MediHealth's pre-existing data.

6. Warranties and Representations
Each Party represents and warrants that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement and will comply with all applicable healthcare laws and regulations.

7. Limitation of Liability
Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. DataCare's total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid in the preceding 12 months.

8. Termination
This Agreement may be terminated by either Party upon 60 days' written notice or for material breach not cured within 30 days.

9. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in Chicago, IL.

10. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Illinois.

11. Miscellaneous
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
