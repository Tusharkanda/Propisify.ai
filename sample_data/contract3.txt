Parties
RetailPro Ltd. ("RetailPro") and MarketGenius Agency ("MarketGenius") (collectively, the "Parties") enter into this Agreement as of August 15, 2025.

1. Term
This Agreement commences on August 15, 2025 and continues until February 15, 2026 unless terminated earlier as provided herein.

2. Scope of Work / Services
MarketGenius will provide digital marketing campaign management, content creation, and analytics services for RetailPro's online store.

3. Payment Terms
RetailPro will pay MarketGenius a total of $60,000, payable in three installments of $20,000 each, due at the start of each campaign phase.

4. Confidentiality
Both Parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all marketing strategies and business data disclosed during the term of this Agreement and for one year thereafter.

5. Intellectual Property
All creative assets developed under this Agreement shall be owned by RetailPro upon full payment.

6. Warranties and Representations
Each Party represents and warrants that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement and will comply with all applicable advertising laws.

7. Limitation of Liability
Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. MarketGenius's total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid.

8. Termination
This Agreement may be terminated by either Party upon 30 days' written notice or for material breach not cured within 10 days.

9. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes shall be resolved by mediation in San Francisco, CA.

10. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

11. Miscellaneous
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
