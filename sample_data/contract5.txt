Parties
EduSmart Technologies ("EduSmart") and LearnOnline Inc. ("LearnOnline") (collectively, the "Parties") enter into this Agreement as of October 1, 2025.

1. Term
This Agreement commences on October 1, 2025 and continues until September 30, 2026 unless terminated earlier as provided herein.

2. Scope of Work / Services
EduSmart will develop and deploy a custom online learning platform for LearnOnline, including analytics and personalization features.

3. Payment Terms
LearnOnline will pay EduSmart $100,000, payable in four equal installments of $25,000 each, due at the completion of each project milestone.

4. Confidentiality
Both Parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all educational content and business information disclosed during the term of this Agreement and for two years thereafter.

5. Intellectual Property
All software and content developed under this Agreement shall be owned by LearnOnline, except for EduSmart's pre-existing tools.

6. Warranties and Representations
Each Party represents and warrants that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement and will comply with all applicable education laws.

7. Limitation of Liability
Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. EduSmart's total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid.

8. Termination
This Agreement may be terminated by either Party upon 30 days' written notice or for material breach not cured within 10 days.

9. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes shall be resolved by arbitration in Austin, TX.

10. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.

11. Miscellaneous
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
